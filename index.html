THE PROJECT

We are building a community to design and create a new concept of sports helmet. We prepared an open innovation platform, and we want you to join us and become part of our Team.

Once the product launches, we will close the platform and every Team member will have a lifelong membership, with a permanent 30% discount on our products. Not enough? Check the rewards section!

THE HELMET

"One helmet to rule them all" and this is what is, a 2.0 helmet adapted to all kind of sports. The original idea came to us when we wanted to record a snowboard video. We thought, why not to have our smartphone integrated with our helmet? We could listen to music, use our camera, our GPS, or even more, we could use new software to enjoy all this features in our helmet.

Download the product description and requirements to understand better the concept. We just ask you to post a tweet to spread the word.
