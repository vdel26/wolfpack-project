THE PROJECT

We are building a community to design and create a new concept of sports helmet. We prepared an open innovation platform, and we want you to join us and become part of our Team.

Once the product launches, we will close the platform and every Team member will have a lifelong membership, with a permanent 30% discount on our products. Not enough? Check the rewards section!

Pre-orders starting in August 2013

THE HELMET

"One helmet to rule them all" and this is what is, a 2.0 helmet adapted to all kind of sports. The original idea came to us when we wanted to record a snowboard video. We tough, why not to have our smartphone integrated with our helmet? We could listen to music, use our camera, our GPS, or even more, we could develop new software to use all this features in our helmet.

Pre-orders starting in August 2013

THE REWARDS

In addition to the Lifelong Team Membership:

The top 10 most voted ideas in our open innovation platform will receive a free helmet.

The top 50 most voted ideas will receive a Wolfpack t-shirt.

But that's not all, we will be hiring our future engineers and designers among you, so spread the word! You might get a job for you or one of your friends.

Pre-orders starting in August 2013
