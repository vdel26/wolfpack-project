THE PROJECT

The Wolfpack Project is an open innovation project that aims to design and create a new concept of extreme sports helmet.

We have prepared a community to vote and discuss about the design of the helmet.

We want you to join us and become part of our Team. We will reward top contributors!

THE HELMET

One Helmet to rule them all

We are currently building a prototype of the helmet. With this helmet you will be able to integrate your smartphone and use the camera, the GPS or listen to your music.

Our question is... How do you want this helmet to be? Do you want it to look more like a skateboard helmet? Would you appreciate an extra protection? Or do you prefer it to be light and stylish? Are you an engineering student and want to contribute? Or do you just love extreme sports and want to make your opinion heard?

Just download the product description and requirements to better understand the concept! We just ask you to post a tweet to help us spread the word.

THE REWARDS

When you join our open innovation platform, you will become automatically a Wolfpack Team member. When the product launches, every team member will enjoy a permanent 20% discount in our products.

In addition to the permanent Team Membership, we will reward top contributors:

The top 10 most voted ideas in our open innovation platform will receive a free helmet.

The top 20 most voted ideas will receive a Wolfpack t-shirt.

But that's not all, we will be hiring our future engineers and designers among you, so spread the word! You might get a job for you or one of your friends.
